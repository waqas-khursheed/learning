# AI Prompt Engineering Roadmap

Ye folder is liye bana hai taake aap seekh saken:
1. AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, koi bhi LLM) ko sahi tareeqe se use karna
2. Achy prompts likhna — jo pehli dafa mein sahi, useful jawab dein
3. AI ko apni coding, learning, aur career improve karne ke liye tool ki tarah use karna
4. Apne aap ko roz behtar bananay ke liye AI se sahi sawal poochna

## Golden Rules (short summary)

1. **Vague mat pucho.** "Laravel sikhao" kaam nahi karega. "Laravel 12 ka Service Container concept beginner level pe example ke sath samjhao" kaam karega.
2. **Context zaroor do.** AI ko pata nahi hota aap kya use kar rahe ho jab tak aap na batao — version, tech stack, level (beginner/intermediate).
3. **Role assign karo.** "Tum ek senior developer ho" bolne se AI ka tone aur depth better hoti hai.
4. **Output format bolo.** Chahte ho code? bullet points? table? step-by-step? Ye clearly likho.
5. **Ek dafa mein perfect answer expect mat karo.** Follow-up karo, refine karo — prompting ek conversation hai, ek order nahi.
6. **Reuse karo.** Har baar naye se likhne ki jagah, [04_ready_templates.md](04_ready_templates.md) se template copy karke sirf blanks fill karo.
