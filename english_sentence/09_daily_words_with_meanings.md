# Daily Words With Meanings (Vocabulary Booster)

Ye file un **words** ke liye hai jo aap roz English bolte waqt use kar sakte ho. Har word ka Roman Urdu meaning aur ek example sentence diya gaya hai — taake aap word ko turant context mein bhi samajh sako.

**Note:** Agar aapko software/work-specific words chahiye (jaise Deploy, Bug, Deadline), wo already [english_course/02_vocabulary_building.md](../english_course/02_vocabulary_building.md) mein hain. Ye file general aur "sound more fluent" words ke liye hai.

## Word Ko Practice Kaise Karo (Sentences Ke Sath)

Sirf word aur uska meaning rat lena kaafi nahi — word ko **istemal (use)** karna aata hona chahiye. Ye tareeqa follow karo:

### Step 1 — Ek Din Mein 8-10 Words Choose Karo
Upar wali list se ek category choose karo (jaise "Action Verbs"). Roz ek category ke 8-10 words lo — poori list ek din mein mat rato.

### Step 2 — Diya Gaya Example Sentence 3 Baar Zor Se Bolo
Har word ke sath jo example sentence diya gaya hai, usay zor se (loud) 3 baar bolo. Isse word ki sahi jagah (sentence mein kahan aata hai) zehen mein baith jati hai.

### Step 3 — Word Ko Purani Sentence Files Ke Sentences Mein Fit Karo
Ab is folder ki pehli 8 files ([01](01_daily_routine_sentences.md) se [08](08_common_idioms_expressions.md) tak) mein jao aur wahan ke kisi sentence mein naya word daal kar sentence ko thora badlo. Jaise:

- Purana sentence (file 03 se): *"I've completed 70% of the task."*
- Naye word "Ensure" ke sath: *"I will ensure this task is completed today."*

- Purana sentence (file 01 se): *"I have a lot of work to do today."*
- Naye word "Prioritize" ke sath: *"I need to prioritize my work today."*

Isse aap purane sentences ko revise bhi kar lete ho aur naye words bhi practice ho jate hain — dono ek sath.

### Step 4 — Apni Zindagi Se Apna Sentence Banao
Ab bilkul apna khud ka sentence banao, apni real life ki koi baat use kar ke. Jaise agar word "Confident" hai:
> "I feel confident about tomorrow's meeting with the client."

### Step 5 — Record Karo Aur Sunno
Apne banaye hue naye sentences ko phone par record karo, sunno, aur pronunciation check karo.

### Step 6 — Agle Din Revise Karo
Kal ke words aaj bhi ek baar bolo (revision), phir naye 8-10 words add karo. Is tarah words bhoolte nahi.

## Weekly Target

| Din | Kaam |

| Din 5 | Connectors (Section 3) practice karo |

| Din 7 | Filler Phrases (Section 5) + poore hafte ke words revise karo |
