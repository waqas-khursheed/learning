# Phone Call Aur Meeting Sentences

## Phone Call Shuru Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Hello, may I speak to Mr. Ahmed, please? | Hello, kya mai Mr. Ahmed se baat kar sakta hoon? |
| Hi, this is Waqas calling from the development team. | Hi, mai Waqas hoon, development team se call kar raha hoon. |
| Is this a good time to talk? | Kya abhi baat karne ka sahi waqt hai? |
| I'm calling regarding the project update. | Mai project update ke bare mein call kar raha hoon. |
| Could you hold on for a second? | Kya aap ek second hold kar sakte hain? |
| Sorry, can you speak a little louder? | Maafi chahta hoon, thora zor se bol sakte hain? |
| The line isn't very clear. | Line saaf nahi hai. |
| Let me call you back in five minutes. | Mai aapko paanch minute mein wapas call karta hoon. |

## Call Ke Dauran

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Can you hear me okay? | Kya aap mujhe theek se sun rahe hain? |
| Sorry, I got disconnected. | Maafi chahta hoon, call cut ho gayi thi. |
| Let me put you on speaker. | Mai aapko speaker par daal deta hoon. |
| I'll send you the details right after this call. | Mai ye call khatam hote hi aapko details bhej dunga. |
| Just to confirm what we discussed... | Sirf confirm karne ke liye jo humne discuss kiya... |
| Could you send that in an email as well? | Kya aap ye email mein bhi bhej sakte hain? |
| I think we have a bad connection. | Mera khayal hai connection kharab hai. |
| Let's continue this conversation over email. | Chalo ye baat email par continue karte hain. |

## Call Khatam Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Thanks for your time, talk soon. | Waqt dene ka shukriya, jald baat hogi. |
| I'll follow up with you tomorrow. | Mai kal aapse follow up karunga. |
| It was great talking to you. | Aap se baat kar ke accha laga. |
| I have another call, I need to go. | Meri ek aur call hai, mujhe jana hoga. |
| Let's wrap this up for now. | Chalo ise abhi ke liye yahin khatam karte hain. |
| Take care, bye for now. | Apna khayal rakhein, filhal ke liye bye. |

## Meeting Shuru Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Let's get started with today's meeting. | Chalo aaj ki meeting shuru karte hain. |
| Thank you all for joining today. | Aaj shamil hone ka sab ka shukriya. |
| Let's go over the agenda first. | Pehle agenda dekh lete hain. |
| Is everyone here? | Kya sab log yahan hain? |
| We're still waiting for one more person to join. | Hum abhi bhi ek aur banday ka intezar kar rahe hain. |

## Meeting Ke Dauran

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Let's move to the next item on the agenda. | Chalo agenda ke agle item par chalte hain. |
| Does anyone have any questions so far? | Ab tak kisi ka koi sawal hai? |
| I'd like to add something here. | Mai yahan kuch add karna chahta hoon. |
| Can we come back to this point later? | Kya hum is point par baad mein wapas aa sakte hain? |
| I think we're running out of time. | Mera khayal hai humara waqt khatam ho raha hai. |
| Let's take this offline and discuss separately. | Chalo ise yahan chhor kar alag se discuss karte hain. |
| Can someone take notes for this meeting? | Kya koi is meeting ke notes le sakta hai? |

## Meeting Khatam Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Let's summarize what we discussed today. | Chalo aaj jo discuss kiya uska khulasa karte hain. |
| I'll share the action items after this meeting. | Mai is meeting ke baad action items share karunga. |
| Thanks everyone for your time today. | Sab ka aaj waqt dene ka shukriya. |
| Let's schedule the next meeting for next week. | Chalo agli meeting agle hafte ke liye schedule karte hain. |
| That's all for today, thank you. | Aaj ke liye bas itna hi, shukriya. |
