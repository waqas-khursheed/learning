# Feelings, Opinions Aur Agree/Disagree

## Feelings Express Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I'm feeling really happy today. | Aaj mai bohot khush mehsoos kar raha hoon. |
| I'm a bit stressed about the deadline. | Deadline ki wajah se mai thora stressed hoon. |
| I feel confident about this project. | Mujhe is project ke bare mein confidence hai. |
| I'm exhausted after today's work. | Aaj ke kaam ke baad mai thak gaya hoon. |
| I'm really excited about this opportunity. | Mai is mauqe se bohot excited hoon. |
| I feel a bit nervous before meetings. | Meetings se pehle mujhe thori nervousness hoti hai. |
| I'm proud of what we achieved. | Humne jo hasil kiya us par mujhe fakhar hai. |
| I'm frustrated with this bug. | Ye bug mujhe frustrate kar raha hai. |
| I feel motivated to finish this today. | Mujhe ye aaj khatam karne ka motivation mehsoos ho raha hai. |
| I'm a little worried about the results. | Mujhe results ke bare mein thori fikar hai. |

## Apni Raay Dena (Giving Opinion)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| In my opinion, this is the best approach. | Meri raay mein, ye behtareen tareeqa hai. |
| I think we should go with this option. | Mera khayal hai humein ye option choose karna chahiye. |
| Personally, I would prefer the second one. | Zaati tor par, mai doosra wala prefer karunga. |
| I believe this will work well. | Mera yaqeen hai ye achhi tarah kaam karega. |
| From my point of view, this makes more sense. | Mere nazariye se, ye zyada sahi lagta hai. |
| I'm not really sure about this. | Mujhe is bare mein zyada yaqeen nahi hai. |
| It seems like a good idea to me. | Mujhe ye ek achha idea lagta hai. |

## Agree Karna

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I completely agree with you. | Mai aapse poori tarah mutafiq hoon. |
| That's exactly what I was thinking. | Ye bilkul wahi hai jo mai soch raha tha. |
| You have a good point. | Aapki baat mein wazan hai. |
| I couldn't agree more. | Mai is se zyada mutafiq nahi ho sakta. |
| That makes sense to me. | Ye mujhe theek lagta hai. |
| Yes, that's true. | Haan, ye sach hai. |

## Disagree Karna (Politely)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I see your point, but I think differently. | Mujhe aapki baat samajh aati hai, lekin mera khayal alag hai. |
| I'm not sure I agree with that. | Mujhe yaqeen nahi ke mai is se mutafiq hoon. |
| I understand, but let me offer a different perspective. | Mujhe samajh aata hai, lekin mujhe ek alag nazariya pesh karne dein. |
| That's a fair point, but there's another way to look at it. | Ye theek baat hai, lekin isay dekhne ka ek aur tareeqa bhi hai. |
| I respectfully disagree. | Mai ehtiraman ittefaq nahi karta. |
| Actually, I had a different experience. | Asal mein, mera tajurba alag tha. |

## Comfort/Support Dena

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| Don't worry, everything will be fine. | Fikar mat karein, sab theek ho jayega. |
| I understand how you feel. | Mujhe samajh aata hai aap kaisa mehsoos kar rahe hain. |
| It happens to everyone sometimes. | Ye kabhi kabhi sab ke sath hota hai. |
| You did a great job. | Aapne bohot achha kaam kiya. |
| Take your time, there's no rush. | Apna waqt lein, koi jaldi nahi. |
| I'm here if you need any help. | Agar aapko madad chahiye ho to mai yahan hoon. |
| You'll figure it out. | Aap samajh jayenge (raasta nikal lenge). |
