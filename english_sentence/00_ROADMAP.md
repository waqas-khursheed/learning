## Kaise Practice Karo (Zaroori)

Sirf parhna kaafi nahi — sentences **zubaan se bolna** zaroori hai. Har file ke liye ye tareeqa follow karo:

1. **Parho** — sentence aur uska Roman Urdu meaning dono parho, samjho.
2. **Zor se bolo** — har sentence ko kam se kam 3 baar zor se (loud) bolo, jaise kisi se baat kar rahe ho.
3. **Apna banao** — sentence ko thora change kar ke apni life pe apply karo. Jaise "I woke up at 7 AM" ko apne actual time ke sath badlo.
4. **Record karo** — kabhi kabhi phone pe apni awaz record karo aur sunno, farq mehsoos hoga.
5. **Roz revise karo** — kal ke sentences aaj bhi ek baar bolo, phir naye add karo.

## Daily Target

- Roz kam se kam **1 file ke 10-15 sentences** choose karo aur unhe zor se bol kar practice karo.
- Ek hafte mein 1 poori file cover ho jayegi.
- 2 mahine mein aap ke paas **200+ ready sentences** honge jo aap kisi bhi conversation mein use kar sakte ho.
