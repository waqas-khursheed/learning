# Daily Routine Sentences (Subah Se Raat Tak)

## Subah (Morning)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I wake up at 7 AM every day. | Mai roz subah 7 baje uthta hoon. |
| I brush my teeth first thing in the morning. | Mai subah sabse pehle brush karta hoon. |
| I take a shower before breakfast. | Mai nashte se pehle nahata hoon. |
| I usually have tea and toast for breakfast. | Mai aksar nashte mein chai aur toast leta hoon. |
| I get ready for work by 8:30. | Mai 8:30 tak kaam ke liye tayar ho jata hoon. |
| I check my phone as soon as I wake up. | Uthte hi mai apna phone check karta hoon. |
| It's a beautiful morning today. | Aaj subah bohot khoobsurat hai. |
| I don't have much time in the morning. | Mere paas subah zyada waqt nahi hota. |
| I skipped breakfast today. | Aaj maine nashta nahi kiya. |
| I need a cup of coffee to wake up properly. | Mujhe sahi tarah jagne ke liye ek cup coffee chahiye. |

## Kaam Par Jana (Going to Work)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I leave for work at 9 AM. | Mai 9 baje kaam ke liye niklta hoon. |
| It takes me 30 minutes to reach the office. | Mujhe office pohanchne mein 30 minute lagte hain. |
| I work from home most days. | Mai zyada tar din ghar se kaam karta hoon. |
| Traffic was really bad today. | Aaj traffic bohot zyada tha. |
| I'm running a bit late today. | Aaj mujhe thori der ho rahi hai. |
| I'll be there in ten minutes. | Mai das minute mein wahan pohanch jaunga. |
| I just left the house. | Mai abhi ghar se nikla hoon. |

## Din Ke Dauran (During the Day)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I have a lot of work to do today. | Aaj mujhe bohot sara kaam karna hai. |
| Let's take a short break. | Chalo thori der break le lete hain. |
| I'm getting hungry, let's have lunch. | Mujhe bhook lag rahi hai, chalo lunch kar lete hain. |
| I need to focus on this task. | Mujhe is task par focus karna hai. |
| I'll finish this in a couple of hours. | Mai ye do ghanton mein complete kar dunga. |
| I'm feeling a bit tired right now. | Abhi mujhe thori thakan mehsoos ho rahi hai. |
| Can we talk about this later? | Kya hum is bare mein baad mein baat kar sakte hain? |
| I lost track of time. | Mujhe waqt ka pata hi nahi chala. |

## Shaam (Evening)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I finish work around 6 PM. | Mai taqreeban 6 baje kaam khatam karta hoon. |
| I like to relax after work. | Mujhe kaam ke baad relax karna pasand hai. |
| I usually go for a walk in the evening. | Mai aksar shaam ko walk pe jata hoon. |
| Let's meet up this evening. | Chalo aaj shaam ko milte hain. |
| I'm just winding down for the day. | Mai bas din ka aakhri hissa relax kar rha hoon. |
| I have some free time this evening. | Aaj shaam mujhe kuch free time hai. |

## Raat (Night)

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| I usually have dinner at 9 PM. | Mai aksar 9 baje dinner karta hoon. |
| I like to read before bed. | Mujhe sone se pehle parhna pasand hai. |
| I go to bed around 11 PM. | Mai taqreeban 11 baje sota hoon. |
| I couldn't sleep well last night. | Mujhe kal raat theek se neend nahi aayi. |
| I'm going to call it a day. | Mai ab aaj ka din yahin khatam karta hoon. |
| Good night, see you tomorrow. | Good night, kal milte hain. |
| I need to set an alarm for tomorrow. | Mujhe kal ke liye alarm lagana hai. |

## Weather Aur General Talk

| English | Roman Urdu Meaning |
|---|---|
| It's really hot today. | Aaj bohot garmi hai. |
| It looks like it's going to rain. | Aisa lagta hai barish hone wali hai. |
| The weather is quite pleasant today. | Aaj mausam kaafi suhana hai. |
| It's freezing cold outside. | Bahar bohot sardi hai. |
| I hope the weather gets better tomorrow. | Umeed hai kal mausam behtar ho jayega. |
