# Phase 5: Client Communication English (Main Focus)

**Goal:** Client k sath emails, chat, calls, aur meetings me confidently professional English use karna.

Ye file sabse important hai — isay bar bar parho, phrases yaad karo, aur real conversations me use karo.

---

## 1. Pehla Message / Introduction

Jab naye client se baat shuru karo:

> "Hi [Name], I hope you're doing well. I'm [Your Name], a Laravel/PHP developer. I saw your project requirements and I believe I can help you with this."

> "Thank you for reaching out. I'd love to discuss your project in more detail."

---

## 2. Project Discuss Karna (Requirements)

- "Could you please share more details about the project requirements?"
- "What is the expected timeline for this project?"
- "Do you have any existing design or documentation I can review?"
- "Just to confirm, you want [feature] to work like this, correct?"
- "Let me make sure I understand this correctly..."

---

## 3. Estimate / Timeline Dena

- "Based on the requirements, I estimate this will take about [X] days/weeks."
- "I can complete the first version by [date]."
- "This feature might take a bit longer because [reason]."
- "I'll need about 2-3 days to give you an accurate estimate."

---

## 4. Progress Update Dena (Bohot Important — Roz ka Kaam)

- "Here's a quick update on the project."
- "I have completed the login and registration module."
- "I'm currently working on the payment integration."
- "This part is almost done, I'll share it with you by tomorrow."
- "I ran into a small issue, but I'm working on a solution."

**Template for daily/weekly update:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Here's today's progress update:
- Completed: [task]
- In Progress: [task]
- Next: [task]

Let me know if you have any questions.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 5. Delay Ho Jaye To Kaise Bataye

Sach batana zaroori hai, lekin professional tareeqe se:

- "I wanted to update you that this task will take a bit longer than expected."
- "I apologize for the delay. I ran into an unexpected issue with [reason], and I'm resolving it now."
- "I will need one more day to complete this properly."
- "Thank you for your patience, I want to make sure this is done correctly."

**Avoid:** Bohot zyada excuses ya baar baar "sorry" mat bolo — solution-focused raho.

---

## 6. Bug / Problem Explain Karna

- "I found an issue where the page doesn't load correctly on mobile."
- "There seems to be a conflict between these two features."
- "I've identified the root cause and I'm fixing it now."
- "This should be resolved within the next hour."

---

## 7. Payment / Price Discussion

- "The total cost for this project would be $[amount]."
- "This additional feature is outside the original scope, so it would cost extra."
- "Could we discuss the payment terms before starting?"
- "I'd suggest splitting the payment into milestones: 50% upfront and 50% on completion."
- "I haven't received the payment yet, could you please check on that?"

---

## 8. Meeting/Call Phrases

### Call shuru karte waqt:
- "Hi, thanks for joining the call."
- "Can you hear me clearly?"
- "Let's go through the project updates."

### Samajh na aaye to (bohot important):
- "Sorry, could you repeat that please?"
- "Could you please speak a bit slower?"
- "Just to clarify, did you mean...?"
- "Sorry, the connection was a bit unclear, could you say that again?"

### Apni baat clearly rakhne k liye:
- "I think it would be better if we..."
- "From a technical point of view, I'd suggest..."
- "That might cause an issue because..."
- "Let me share my screen and show you."

### Call end karte waqt:
- "So just to summarize, I'll complete [task] by [date]."
- "I'll send you a written summary after this call."
- "Thanks for your time, talk to you soon."

---

## 9. Difficult Situations (Handle Karna)

### Client naraz ho:
- "I understand your concern, and I apologize for the inconvenience."
- "Let me fix this right away."
- "I want to make this right for you."

### Scope badal jaye (client zyada maang le):
- "This is a great idea, but it's outside our original agreement. I can add it as an additional task."
- "I'm happy to work on this, but it will affect the timeline/cost. Is that okay with you?"

### Aap ko kuch samajh na aaye (requirement clear na ho):
- "Could you provide an example of what you're looking for?"
- "A screenshot or reference link would really help me understand better."

---

## 10. Polite Business Phrases (Overall Tone)

| Direct/Rude sounding | Polite/Professional |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| "Send me the file." | "Could you please send me the file?" |
| "I don't know." | "Let me check and get back to you." |
| "It's not possible." | "This might be a bit challenging, but let's see what we can do." |
| "You are wrong." | "I see it a bit differently, let me explain." |
| "Pay me first." | "Could we agree on the payment terms before I start?" |

---

## 11. Real Practice Exercise

Har hafte ek mock scenario khud se (ya kisi dost se) practice karo:
1. Naye client ko apna intro do (video record karo)
2. Ek fake project ka progress update likho
3. Ek delay ka email likho aur explain karo
4. Ek call scenario imagine karo jahan client tez bolta hai aur aap usay clarify karne k liye kehte ho
