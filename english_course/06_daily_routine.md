# Phase 6: Daily Practice Routine (Ongoing)

Ab sab phases combine karke ek roz ka schedule follow karo. Total time: **45-60 minutes/day**.

---

## Daily Schedule (45-60 min)

| Time | Activity | File Reference |
|------|----------|------------------|
| 10 min | Naye 10 words yaad karo + sentence banao | [02_vocabulary_building.md](02_vocabulary_building.md) |
| 15 min | Shadowing practice (video k sath bolna) | [03_speaking_practice.md](03_speaking_practice.md) |
| 10 min | Listening (3-pass technique) | [04_listening_practice.md](04_listening_practice.md) |
| 5 min | Mirror me apna din describe karo | [03_speaking_practice.md](03_speaking_practice.md) |
| 5-10 min | Client communication phrases revise/practice karo | [05_client_communication.md](05_client_communication.md) |

---

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Monday: Naye tense/grammar topic revise karo
- [ ] Tuesday-Thursday: Roz ki routine (upar wali table)
- [ ] Friday: Ek mock client scenario practice karo (email/call script)
- [ ] Saturday: Ek podcast/video suno full attention se, notes lo
- [ ] Sunday: Poore hafte k naye words revise karo, weak areas identify karo

---

## Progress Tracking

Har hafte khud se ye 3 sawal poocho:
1. Kya mujhe apna kaam (project) English me explain karna aasan lag raha hai?
2. Kya main client ka message/email bina atkay samajh pa raha hoon?
3. Kya main bina zyada soche sentence bana pa raha hoon?

Jab teenon k jawab "haan" hone lagen, to samjho aap client-ready ho chuke ho.

---

## Milestones (Kab kya expect karo)

| Time | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2 weeks | Basic tenses aur sentence structure comfortable |
| 1 month | 300+ practical words yaad, roz ki baatein English me kar sako |
| 2 months | Emails aur chat messages confidently likh sako |
| 3 months | Calls/meetings me bina zyada atkay baat kar sako |

**Consistency sabse zaroori hai — roz thora karna, kabhi kabhi bohot karne se behtar hai.**
