# Phase 1: Grammar Basics

**Goal:** Itni grammar seekh lo k aap sahi tense me, sahi sentence structure me baat kar sako. Perfect grammar ka target mat rakho — clear communication target rakho.

---

## 1. Sentence ka Basic Structure

English me hamesha ye order follow hota hai:

```
Subject + Verb + Object
(I)     + (eat) + (food)
```

Urdu me hum "Main khana khata hoon" bolte hain (Subject + Object + Verb),
English me: "I eat food" (Subject + Verb + Object).

**Ye sabse common mistake hai jo Urdu speakers karte hain — Urdu order me English bolna.**
Practice: pehle Subject bolo, phir Verb, phir baaki sentence.

---

## 2. Tenses — Sirf Wahi Jo Rozana Chahiye

Aapko saari 12 tenses rat'ne ki zaroorat nahi. Ye 6 sabse zyada use hoti hain:

### a) Present Simple — Routine/facts k liye
`Subject + verb (s/es with he/she/it)`
- I work on Laravel projects.
- She checks the code daily.
- The client sends feedback every week.

### b) Present Continuous — Abhi ho raha kaam
`Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing`
- I am working on the login page.
- We are fixing the bug right now.

### c) Past Simple — Jo ho chuka
`Subject + verb-2 (past form)`
- I completed the task yesterday.
- The client approved the design.

### d) Present Perfect — Jo abhi complete hua, result important hai
`Subject + have/has + verb-3 (past participle)`
- I have finished the API integration.
- The client has sent the payment.

### e) Future Simple — Aage kya hoga
`Subject + will + verb`
- I will send the update by tomorrow.
- We will deploy the project next week.

### f) Future with "going to" — Plan/intention
`Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb`
- I am going to fix this issue today.

## 3. Common Mistakes (Urdu Speakers)

| Galat | Sahi | Wajah |
|-------|------|-------|
| "I am agree" | "I agree" | "Agree" verb hai, "am" ki zaroorat nahi |
| "She is knowing English" | "She knows English" | Stative verbs (know, like, want) continuous me nahi aate |
| "I have 25 years" | "I am 25 years old" | Age k liye "have" nahi "am" use hota hai |
| "Kindly do the needful" | "Please do this" ya specific request | Ye purani formal Indian-English phrase hai, ab natural nahi lagti |
| "Revert back" | "Reply" ya "Get back to you" | "Revert" already "wapis" ka matlab rakhta hai |

## 4. Question Banana

- **Yes/No question:** Do/Does/Is/Are + Subject + verb?
  - Do you have the updated file?
  - Is the server running?

- **Wh- question:** What/When/Where/Why/How + do/does/is + Subject + verb?
  - When will you send the update?
  - Why is the page loading slowly?
