# English Learning Roadmap (Client Communication k liye)

Ye course is liye bana hai k aap:
1. Roz-marra English samajh aur bol saken
2. Client k sath professional English me baat kar saken (emails, calls, meetings)
3. Confidence k sath apna kaam/project explain kar saken

## Golden Rules

1. **Roz kam se kam 30-45 min do.** Ek ghanta ek din se behtar hai 30 min roz.
2. **Likhna, parhna, sunna, bolna** — chaaron cheezein sath sath karo, sirf grammar rat kar English nahi aati.
3. **Mistakes se mat daro.** Client bhi samajhta hai ke aap non-native speaker ho — clarity important hai, perfection nahi.
4. **Apne kaam (Laravel/PHP/Web Dev) ki English pehle seekho** — general English baad me, kyunke aapko client se yehi baat karni hai.
5. **Har cheez zor se bolo (loud), sirf zehen me mat parho.** Bolne ki practice sirf bolne se aati hai.

## Sequence — Pehle Kya, Phir Kya

```
Step 1: Grammar basics samjho (tenses + sentence structure)
   |
Step 2: Roz use hone wale words yaad karo (vocabulary)
   |
Step 3: Un words/sentences ko zor se bolna practice karo (speaking)
   |
Step 4: English sunna shuru karo (videos/podcasts) — samajhna
   |
Step 5: Client communication phrases seekho — emails, calls, meetings
   |
Step 6: Sab kuch ek daily routine me combine karo aur consistently practice karo
```
