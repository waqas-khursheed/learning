# Phase 3: Speaking Practice

**Goal:** Zehen me sochi hui English ko zubaan tak la kar bolna. Ye sabse mushkil phase hai kyunke yahan "sochna" aur "bolna" dono sath karna parta hai.

---

## 1. Shadowing Technique (Sabse Best Method)

**Shadowing** ka matlab hai kisi native speaker ki video/audio sunte hue, saath saath wahi words bolna (ek dam saath, ya thora sa peechay).

### Kaise karo:
1. Koi 1-2 minute ka English video dhoondo (YouTube - simple English, jaise tech tutorials ya news).
2. Pehle bina rukay poora sun lo, samjho.
3. Ab video dobara chalao aur speaker k sath sath, same time par, wahi words bolna shuru karo.
4. Isay 5-10 baar repeat karo, jab tak fluent na lagay.

**Roz 15-20 minutes shadowing karo — ye pronunciation aur rhythm dono improve karta hai.**

---

## 2. Mirror Practice

Aaine k samne khare ho kar roz 5 minutes apne din k baray me English me bolo:
- "Today I worked on..."
- "I fixed a bug in..."
- "Tomorrow I will..."

Apni body language aur confidence bhi dekho jab bolo.

---

## 3. Self-Recording

Apna phone use karke apni awaz record karo (30 second - 1 minute) kisi bhi topic par:
- "Explain your current project in English."
- "Describe your day."

Phir sunno — jahan atak rahe ho ya galti ho rahi hai wo nishan lagao. Agle din wahi topic phir se record karo, farq mehsoos hoga.

---

## 4. Pronunciation — Common Problem Sounds (Urdu speakers k liye)

| Sound | Problem | Practice Words |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| "th" | Aksar "t" ya "d" ban jata hai | think, three, this, that |
| "v" vs "w" | Mix ho jate hain | very, wine, vine, west |
| "-ed" ending | Extra "id" sound add ho jata hai | worked (vs "work-id"), fixed |
| Word stress | Galat syllable par zor | de-VEL-op, com-PUT-er |

**Tip:** YouTube par "th sound practice" ya "v and w sound difference" search karo, 5 minute wale videos milenge — unhe follow karo.

---

## 5. Think in English (Zaroori Habit)

Jab bhi koi cheez soch rahe ho (jaise "aaj mujhe ye task karna hai"), usay Urdu me sochne k bajaye seedha English me sochne ki koshish karo:
> "I need to complete this task today."

Shuru me mushkil hoga, lekin 2-3 hafton me zehen khud English me sochne lagta hai.

---

## 6. Daily Speaking Routine

1. **5 min** — Mirror practice (apna din describe karo)
2. **15 min** — Shadowing (video k sath bolna)
3. **5 min** — Self-recording aur sunna
4. **Poore din** — Chotay chotay jumlay English me sochne ki koshish karo
